A full walk of every module, the storage layer and the last roadmap. Verdict: the visible product is nearly done and holding up well — the gaps that remain are almost all in the data foundation: what survives a reload, what happens on a second tab, and what a brand-new business sees on day one.

Module condition report— A SOLID · B GAPS · C NEEDS WORK
Ledger & correctionsB+
Deep and correct — but posted deals don't survive a reload; the book re-seeds. See the red flag below.
New Transaction · quote loopA
Quote → confirm → receipt with inline KYC and customer display. Signature capture still deferred.
Compliance & FilingsA−
Screening, 24h aggregation, structuring watch, sealed filings. Last mile: a real FINTRAC pipe.
Clients · KYCB+
ID scan → autofill, expiry flags, Persona flow (simulated). ID photos live inline in a ~5 MB browser store — quota bomb.
TransfersB+
Beneficiaries, corridors, lifecycle, partner settlement, EFT reports. Settlement doesn't move till/vault cash yet.
ChequesB+
Fronting-risk model with holds & NSF is right. Clearing is hand-advanced; a returned cheque's loss posts as a note, not a ledger entry.
Till & DrawerA−
Blind counts, shift handoff, variance log. Ageing (stale counts, holds) is blunted by the frozen demo clock.
VaultB
Position & realized-margin math holds up. Wholesale receipts are manual; no vault↔till transfer document.
BranchesB
The station model is the OS backbone. Inter-branch movements are recorded but not enforced against till floats.
DashboardA
Banknote plate, live KPIs, compliance tiles, dark variant. Nothing blocking.
ReportsA−
Signed Z-report, P&L, access matrix. Tax assumptions fixed; no scheduled generation or delivery.
Pricing & RatesA
Spread console, guardrails with reason-coded overrides, one-click publish. Done.
Rate Board (embed)B
Lives in its own iframe with its own styling; talks to the OS via storage only and ignores the app theme (its black board reads fine in both).
Settings · Store · BillingA−
Deep config, plans & app gating, invoices. Payments simulated; appearance control shipped here.
OS shell · windows · search · auditA−
Windowing, dock editing, spotlight, audit trail are strong. No cross-tab sync — two tabs clobber each other, last writer wins.
Assistant · CalculatorsB
Desk-aware AI chat, FX/margin/loan calcs. Assistant depends on the host AI bridge — needs graceful degrade elsewhere.
RED FLAG

The data model is split-brained. [Resolved 2026-07-05 — №02 shipped: the book & roster now persist; reset is an explicit Settings control.] Cheques, transfers, filings, till counts, branches and settings all persist — but the ledger book and client roster re-seed on every reload. Post ten deals, refresh, they're gone; the sealed filing that referenced them remains. One afternoon of work (queue #02) makes the whole demo coherent; the real cure is still queue #01. Adjacent risks in the same wall: images stored inline in a ~5 MB budget, 27 storage keys with no versioned migrations, no export, no multi-tab lock, and a clock frozen at 2026-06-18.
